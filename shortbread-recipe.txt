Basic Shortbread Cookies
Ingredients
- 2 cups butter
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Directions
- Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 C)
- Cream butter and brown sugar.
- Add 3 to 4 cups of flour.  Mix well.
- Sprinkle board with remaining flour.  Knead for 5 mins, adding enough flour to make soft dough.
- Roll to 1/2 inch thickness.
- Cut into 3x1 inch strips.
- Prick with fork and place on ungreased baking sheet.
- Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
